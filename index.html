About

Engineer. Librarian.
Problem Solver.

I'm Alabi Abdulmumuni Kelvin, founder of The Orderly Standard, a web studio that builds fast, structured digital systems for clients across healthcare, consulting, tech, and education, from Nigeria to the UK, USA, and Germany.

I am a problem solver by instinct. I bridge the gap between human needs and digital solutions by bringing two unlikely disciplines together: Information Science as a professional librarian, and self-taught mastery of web engineering. That combination is rare and it is precisely what my clients benefit from.

I have delivered platforms for clients in Nigeria, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Germany. My work is not about what looks good on day one. It is about building systems designed for stability, legacy, and long-term clarity.
